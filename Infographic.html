The Path Forward: Key Recommendations

1. Strengthen Teacher Capacity

Invest in scalable, continuous, and contextualized professional development using blended learning modules and mentorship networks to equip teachers for new pedagogical approaches.

2. Ensure Equitable Resources

Implement targeted funding for infrastructure gaps and develop diverse, open-source learning resources to bridge the digital and material divide, especially in rural areas.

3. Establish Feedback Loops

Create a robust, adaptive assessment framework and a digital platform for real-time feedback from schools to enable continuous improvement and data-informed policy adjustments.
